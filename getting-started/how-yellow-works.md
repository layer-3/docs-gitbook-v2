# How Yellow.pro Works

Yellow.pro combines fast trading with flexible account access. The platform supports two ways to get started and uses different balance areas depending on your account type and trading activity.

## Ways to Access Yellow.pro

You can access Yellow.pro using either method:

* **Connect an external wallet** — Link a Web3 wallet like MetaMask, Rabby, Phantom, or WalletConnect directly to the platform.
* **Sign in with Google** — Yellow.pro automatically creates a Yellow Wallet linked to your account for easy access.

Both methods provide the same trading features and platform access.

## Understanding Your Balance Areas

Yellow.pro uses different balance areas depending on the feature you're using:

| Balance type | Purpose |
| --- | --- |
| **Account Balance** (`Yellow Wallet`) | Main balance used for deposits and withdrawals by Google Sign-in users. This is your Yellow Wallet. |
| **Trading Account** (`Spot Account`) | Balance used for Spot trading. |
| **Perpetual Account** | Balance used for Perpetual trading positions. |

Understanding where your funds are located is important, because deposits, transfers, trading, and withdrawals all depend on which balance currently holds your assets.

## Supported Products

* **Spot Trading** — Buy and sell assets at current market prices, or use limit orders for more control.
* **Perpetual Trading** — Open leveraged positions and trade with exposure to asset price movements.
* **Blockchain Deposits & Withdrawals** — Deposit and withdraw directly to and from your wallet on supported networks.

{% hint style="info" %}
**Key takeaway:** Yellow.pro works around a simple principle — connect your account, fund your balance, transfer funds if needed, trade, and withdraw your assets. The exact steps depend on whether you connected an external wallet or signed in with Google.
{% endhint %}
